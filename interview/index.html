Up close and personal with Terra Danecker.

Interview by: Unsia Imtiaz Nov 30,2020

Unsia:If you would have to describe yourself in one sentence what would that be?
Terra:I am 21 college student in the middle of a pandemic who is bored of working at home cause my parents love to come into my room multiple times a day and ask “how are things going?”.
U:Who do you look up to the most? And why?
T:I don’t really go looking people up unless I have to.
U:What would your perfect day would include?
T:A cool fall day walking through the forest with a friend or two. Listening to the birds chirp and the leafs crunch as we walking along talking about anything. Then going home to have a campfire and watching the stars.
U:What’s your biggest fear?
T:Being buried alive.
U:Where do you hope to be in five years from now?
T:I hope to have a stable job. Not working from home! I’m not too sure where maybe somewhere different maybe outside of Canada.
U:When have you felt most proud? Recently?
T:Last year when I got all of my assignments finished and handed in.
U:When was the last time you laughed hard?
T:I don’t remember. It’s been a long time.
U:If you could eat one thing for the rest of your life, what would it be?
T:Beef Stir fry!
U:If you were to get a tattoo, what would that be ?
T:Idk, I just got a new tattoo I don’t think I’ve had time to think about my next one... maybe a cupcake for my grandma
U:If you had to pick one, would that be a dog or a cat ?
T:I would pick a dog, cats are evil plus I’m allergic to them.
U:Do you believe in aliens?
T:YES, we can not be the only living.
U:What college major you chose and why?
T:Graphic design, I am very passionate about art and my creative side and graphic design give my passion a perspective.
U:What era do you prefer 80’s or 90’s and why?
T:80’s because they had better music style.
U:If you were an animal what would you be?
T:A panda.
